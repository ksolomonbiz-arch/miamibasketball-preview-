The legacy

Built by Miami. For Miami.

What started in 1999 with one coach training kids one-on-one became South Florida's first league to let entire teams sign up together — and grew into a full-service sports company producing leagues, tournaments, and corporate events across four counties.

We launched the original summer camp program at Don Shula's. We run city contracts from Doral to Hollywood. We organize sporting scenes for Hollywood productions. And we've been trusted by NBA-affiliated families and South Florida's championship community for over two decades.
